Materials & Finishes

Every mandir we craft begins with one choice — the material that will hold your faith for generations.

We offer six material categories, each with its own character, warmth, and investment level. Whether you prefer the warmth of solid wood, the timelessness of marble, or the modern precision of solid surface — we craft it to perfection.

🪟
Acrylic + Prelam
₹ Entry Range

A glossy acrylic sheet bonded over a pre-laminated base board. Delivers a clean, premium-looking finish at an accessible price point. Available in a wide range of solid colours and soft textures.

Finish
High-gloss, mirror-like
Durability
Good — scratch resistant
Maintenance
Easy, wipe clean
Weight
Light to medium
Best For Apartments, compact spaces, first-time buyers, and those wanting a modern look on a budget.
🎨
HDHMR + Duco Paint
₹₹ Mid Range

High-Density High-Moisture Resistant board finished with automotive-grade Duco paint. Gives a seamless, furniture-grade surface with exceptional colour depth — popular in contemporary interiors.

Finish
Silky smooth, even coat
Durability
Excellent — moisture safe
Maintenance
Easy, minimal upkeep
Customisation
Any RAL / Pantone colour
Best For Modern homes, bungalows, and customers wanting a premium painted look with clean lines.
🪵
Solid Wood
₹₹₹ Premium

Crafted from natural teak, sheesham, or sagwan — solid wood mandirs carry the warmth and grain of real timber. Each piece is unique. Ages beautifully and can be passed down through generations.

Wood Types
Teak · Sheesham · Sagwan
Durability
Heirloom — 50+ years
Finish Options
Natural oil, walnut stain, polish
Carving
Hand-carved or CNC available
Best For Traditional homes, pooja rooms, customers wanting the natural warmth and sacred feel of real wood.
💎
Solid Surface
₹₹₹ Premium

An engineered composite material (like Corian or HI-MACS) that is non-porous, seamless, and highly workable. Allows complex curved forms and intricate CNC detailing while remaining stain and water resistant.

Appearance
Stone-like, seamless
Durability
Very high — renewable surface
Maintenance
Very easy — stain proof
Design Freedom
Curves, arches, inlays
Best For Contemporary fusion designs, bungalow entrances, customers wanting a stone look with modern functionality.
🏛️
Marble
₹₹₹₹ Luxury

The most sacred and timeless choice. We source Makrana white marble (the same marble used in the Taj Mahal), Vietnam white, and Italian Statuario. Each slab is hand-selected and carved by master craftsmen from Rajasthan.

Marble Types
Makrana · Vietnam · Statuario
Durability
Generational —100+ years
Carving
Hand-carved by artisans
Sourcing
Directly from Rajasthan
Best For Dedicated pooja rooms, bungalows, heirloom mandirs, and those who want the finest sacred space possible.
✨
Real Silver
★ Ultra Luxury

A real silver coating applied as the finish on the mandir — the entire exterior is adorned in genuine silver, giving the mandir a divine, temple-like presence. This is not a plating but a true silver finish, handcrafted by specialist artisans. Pricing is fully custom based on size and design complexity.

Finish Type
Real silver coating
Craftsmanship
Handcrafted by artisans
Appearance
Divine, temple-like glow
Pricing
Custom — on consultation
Best For Those who want their mandir to look and feel like a sacred temple — with a genuine silver finish that radiates divinity.
